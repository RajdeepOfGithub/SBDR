This project isn't about "forgiving debt"—it's about **optimizing recovery**. Banks in 2026 are shifting to this model because "Value Banking" and empathy are the only ways to differentiate themselves in a crowded market where everyone offers similar interest rates.


In 2026, the cost of acquiring a new bank customer (CAC) is **5 to 25 times higher** than retaining an existing one.

In our project:
By using the "Compassionate AI" to help a customer through a 3-month rough patch for example, could be health crisis or getting fired from their job, the bank keeps them as a customer for the next 20 years. A **5% increase in retention** can boost overall bank profits by **25% to 95%**.
